I create transcriptions as part of the general process of learning songs. Sometimes they include other parts, mostly just the bass line. The general aim is for me to be able to replicate the correct rhythms of the track, in order to get the right feel in tandem with the drummer. When you have a lot of songs in your repertoire, it's easy for them to start merging into each other! Hence, the scores are aiming for (more or less) note perfection - though when the bass line is heavily improvised on a theme, I may just put a repeat marker.
